Qafarre - Qaada-Madqa

Qafar ummatta abuuy akaakayyuh edde xiinak temeteeh uxih edde xintah tan Qafarre Madqak adda nagay yaaxigeenim dibuh hinnay qasriino le gurral baadal aysixxiguk nagrisaanama!

Qafarre Macaay?

Qafarre deqsittam Qafar ummatta abuuy akaakayyuh qunxxah kaxxam edde xiinissaah, umaane edde kalaltaah, toomem edde taymaaqeh tan abuk raqti 'Ganuunuy' mango daboonah ankuttube kal makaabon aliilih luk sugte.

Awayih uddur tunkuttubem dubuh hinnay Qasriinot culusak Galli Marok UNSCO (Yuniskoh) mamaxxaga kah geytannah tu taaxago kee doolat xagorti elle taamitak geytimta caddo gufteh geytimta. Ta madqay amolladih Qafarrel tamixxigeeh, addal kaaduk darifaak dariifal dagoo baxsa luk 5 takkek addafakoot ta Weebsayitil geetton. Ta Madqay nek dumi mari ankuttube kal horak horal tabk nee gufteh tani, nanu kaadu asaaku edde tan caddok muxxi kusaaqittey, qasriino kee teknoolojit antifiquk dadlisnaah, baye kallam faxximtam kassisna.

Arrabak xabba haanam 5 feerak sehdaytul bahen baatil timixxige digaala le.

Digaala Takkuqem Kedo

Qafarrel, faxe baatil kee hoxak digaala (qaaqa) elle raddam dubuh hoxe num hinnay kah yaabuke kedo.

Idoolâ Doori

Qafarrel faxe malkitik margaqa beytam Makaabonul tamixxige idoolay ayyunti addal kaxxa massakaxxa le.

Cokmi Gexsit

Cokmi elle yakkem duma macak maca iyyenih sugeniih, axcuk madqa akkuk temeete margaqooqi.!

Qadli kee Damiir

Qaffarel Mablo kaxxaam qadlil takke. Umam baahe mara kee elle baahen mara yalla kasak cakkit yaaban.

Qafarre – Qafarak Abuk-Raqti (Madqa)

Qafar Madqooqi sittat 5 takke. Takkay ikkah baxsi keenik kaxxaam dagooh, axcih digaala edde meklan saqih qaynat kee mangak biso baxsa mali. Kulli dariifal mangih lon saqa, axcih Gaala, Laa kee Wadar kinnuk ahaak gubal elle tablen innal tan.

Areas Governed by Mada'a

Assault & Injury

Specific compensation amounts are prescribed for different types of injuries, from minor wounds to permanent disability or disfigurement.

Property & Livestock

Rules cover theft, damage to property, grazing rights, water access, and the use of communal resources like pastureland and wells.

Sexual Offenses

Mada'a addresses issues of adultery, rape, and other sexual misconduct, with compensation and social sanctions as primary responses.

Peace Treaties

Formal procedures exist for negotiating and concluding peace agreements between clans, including ritual elements that make the agreement binding.

Camel-Based Compensation • Traditional Afar Customary Law

Burqili Madqal arrabak tewqemik xabba haanam, biyakaay, Garqiinok rooci/Diyata (Biilu) fan takke digaala qasa wadarah meklan.

Note: These are traditional values. Actual compensation may vary by region and specific circumstances.

Qafar Madqooqiy (5)

Akkel Hayneh Nan MadqooqiK Baxxaqsa kee Elle kobxisen inni edde anuk addafak

Culmeena

Qafar ummattaa kee tet ardi, Farangi ku-amol-ayyok wadirillah, sidiica baaxol kurrumteh raqte. Tohim:-

  • 1. Itoobboya
  • 2. Eretriya
  • 3. Jabuuti

Eretriyaa kee Jabuuti namma Farangi (Tilyaan kee faransaawi) baxsitte baaxo kinni waqdi, Itoobboyah tu gactek, Qafar kee Qafar baaxo affara xiinissoh rakaakat kuram kurteh, baxambaxsitteh ten wohim:-

  • 1. Harara
  • 2. Shawa
  • 3. Wollo
  • 4. Tigraaya

Tohim irô gaboobi teetit abte baxambaxsa kinni waqdi, Qafar isaddal, isik isih sissin koona saltanat kee mango kedoodâ xaltak ballisih xiinissoosa kurruumak sugtem (Tribal Confederations) giddi sinni numma.

Itoobboyal affara xiinissoh raakaakayat baxambaxsah sugte Qafar, inki xiinissoh raakaakayay teetil muggaqsime geyyiiy, isi muddaqiyanno konnaba elle daabisseek lakal, Eretriya edde anak koona raakaakayat baxambaxxsah ten loqo, mariiy rikel luk suge madqooqi, sittâ fan gacissi heenih, Qafar madqa akak axcuk affeysaanam faxxinte.

Ta Koona Madqa Elle muden Baaxo

1. Burqili Madqa

Qaynata: Qiddik madqah alayto

Elle muden araca: Dammohoyta, Seekay, Daahim meelaay, Hadar mo

Mudde gaba: A madaqa koobaahissem Burqli

Rasitte kee baaxo: Eretriya, Itoobboya k kilbatti rasu kee 4hayto rasu

2. Buxxuto Baxih Madqa

Qaynata: Mogorr os kee daabuk fanah raqta baaxok rakub caxa xiqista aracal muden

Elle muden araca: Qarabtaay, qasa bakariiy, nassar kee qaginni

Mudde gaba: A madaqa koobaahissem Buxxutô Baxa Casana

Rasitte kee baaxo: Eretriya, Itoobboya k Awsi Rasuuy, Gabbi Rasuuy, Fanti Rasu kee Carri Rasu

3. Afkigek Maqaadih Madqa

Qaynata: Aysaqii tak walaqs andiqsi ta araca

Elle muden araca: Afkigek Maqaadaay, kee Aydaacissooy Qarkaytoh xiinak sugte

Rasitte kee baaxo: Awsi Rasuuy, Gabbi Rasuuy, Fanti Rasu kee Carri Rasu

4. Bodoytâ Meelah Madqa

Qaynata: Qaddaq aarak tewqo xiqsita araca

Elle muden araca: Qali Dawwaani Macammada

Rasitte kee baaxo: Carri Rasu

5. Debnek-Weeqiimih Madqa

Qaynata: Weeqiimi daqaar ak qadaylu xiqsita araca

Elle muden araca: Qali Qablis kee Qali Qarqi Macammada

Rasitte kee baaxo: Jabuuti kee Itoobboyak Gabbi Rasu

Gaaboyso (Sanat 2000-2006)

Ta madqooqi gabat leh yan marak, kutbeh gaaboysaanam faxximte. Too gaaboysoy namma gurral tekke, meqe malcina liggida beyte. Tohum sanat 2000-2006 fan kinni.

Woo gaabooyso, elle tekke namma gurrah tu gacte tet tekkek, madqooqi elle taamitta rasitte fan gexak gaaboysimtem kee, tet abnisak suge marak tut tu, Samara magaalâ fan baahak, esserik-gacsal gaabowtem tekke.

Qafar Madqooqih Kinmaane kee Weeloola

  1. Naharsi weelo: Qafar madqooqi elle tamiixige, baatileyna, abbixekal, axeekal, digaaltu duddaamih duddo.
  2. Nammayheele weelo: Uson elle yamixxigeeni abitto (commission) gorcii kee garci baxisak digaaltaama.
  3. Sidocheele weelo: Qafar madqooqi elle tamiixige, galelway (umaane abaluk cate wayti) digaaltaama.
  4. Fereeyheele weelo: Tet elle yaaxigeeni yekke baatilih, abte gaba dibuk hinnay, inkih tan kedo essertimtaamih tiya.
  5. Konooyheele weelo: Uson elle yamixxigeeni, mabloh daffeyte caxá, urruu kee agabu akke waytek numuk gabah assagolli waaso aalle waytaama aymisen ken tekkek faxeemî may.
  6. Leceeyheele weelo: Qafar madqooqi elle tamixxige, meklah madqan saqa, ufuylii kee ufuy malih nammâ kel baxissaamih tiya. Ufuymali lakqoh korâ kal, ufuyli lakqoh ma kora.
  7. Malcinheele weelo: Qafar madqooqi elle tamixxige abtol asewaaway akke raariyya digaaltaama.

Qafar Madqak Mablö Qexsaya

  1. Fiiruk: Waytó xayyowta
  2. Nammeyhaak: Wayto bahtem loyna faxe waa num tekkek caxah muggaaqisan. Awka tekkek loyna bahit kaak iyyan.
  3. Sidoc haak: Xayyowte waytoh caxá muggaqsimta
  4. Ferey haak: Woo caxah ayroo kee arac muggaqsima
  5. Konyhaak: Maró daffeıta waqdi, leek qayso, waytó le numuk/marak xayıoysâ yan
  6. Leceyhaak: Yab haysite num kee edde numuk inkih mabbaxeyna baaha iyyan.
  7. Malcin haak: Caxal, yab fiiruk waytó le exxah yaceen.
  8. Bacarhaak: Af haysitaah, af kalsitah axcuk, yab tu kak waan exxah yaceen.
  9. Saqal haak: Qagu namma exxah inkih yaceen.
  10. Taban haak: Iyyeenim tah kinnii? Axcuk rade yab gabbaaqan.
  11. Taban kee enekhaak: Rade yab agaaradak makeelisan.
  12. Taban kee nammay haak: Yab wadu faxak raaqe inna? Ken afal yenbexe? Iyyan.
  13. Taban kee sidoc haak: Yab giddih raaqek, sumaq marih yab yaabben.
  14. Taban kee ferey haak: Sumaq waytimek xiiba absiisaanah.
  15. Taban kee konoy haak: Yab haysite num waddiime kaa tekkek margaqa tantacowowe.
  16. Taban kee lecey haak: Yabti margaqa, hayste num kee edde haysiten numul inkih gacsan.
  17. Taban kee malcin haak: Edde cayye wayte exxa yoo-maxiqinna xayyoysan.
  18. Taban kee bacar haak: Edde cabeenim faatacisan.

Digaalá

Ahak naharal mango waqdi; galab kak xagne koona madqay, Qafar ummatta akah xinta; digaalah tascasseeh, madqá elle lem canee kee meklá.

Ceellallo: Num toobokoyta isik qidde kedok num qidek, toh cané deqsitta.

Qafár madqooqil, num qideenik biilu yakke. Biili, niqnal elle canel anfiddigek, xabbacowoheh meklal anfidigge yakke.

Meklá, Qafar madqooqil, sahdayti, sahdaytul baaheemih namma migaq le. Rooci meklá diyat deqsittaah, xagar meklá xikcá deqsitta.

Bilu

Qafar num Qafar numih gabat rabe kaa tekkek, num qidde kedo MEGLÖ may abta áwya le.

Megló addah kak orobte kedo, num kak rabe kedó fan gexak, meglo ken cultem kee, woo buxá nerraay nefdiga axcuk temeetem, ken yaysixxigen.
